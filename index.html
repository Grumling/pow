Our functionality

These non-alcoholic beverages are designed to provide the consumer with a rapid energy boost.

They have far less calories than commercial beverages and provide an energy boost due to the high caffeine level. 


Our product comes in a powder form that you can easily combine with water, wherever you are and whenever you like.

We use 100% recycled plastic for our packaging.
